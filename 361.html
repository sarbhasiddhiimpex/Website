361°

361 Degrees is a sportswear and athletic footwear company from China that designs and sells running shoes, basketball shoes, sports clothing, and accessories. The company is known for offering performance-focused products at more affordable prices compared to many global sports brands.The name “361°” represents going one degree beyond 360°, symbolizing extra effort, passion, and improvement. The brand is especially popular in basketball and running, and it sponsors athletes, sports teams, and international sporting events. Main products include:Running shoes,Basketball shoes,Training shoes,Sportswear and activewear Kids sports products.The company has expanded internationally and is recognized for combining modern sports technology with budget-friendly pricing.
