Toread

Toread Holdings Group is a Chinese outdoor apparel and equipment company founded in 1999 and headquartered in Beijing. The company designs and sells outdoor clothing, hiking gear, camping equipment, footwear, backpacks, and sports accessories under brands such as TOREAD, TOREAD Kids, and TOREAD.X. The brand is known for outdoor activities including hiking, skiing, mountaineering, camping, and travel. It has collaborated with Chinese Antarctic research teams, mountaineering expeditions, and space-related projects, helping build its reputation in China’s outdoor industry.The company also develops proprietary outdoor fabric and safety technologies such as TiEF and SAfree for weather protection and comfort. In recent years, Toread expanded beyond outdoor products into semiconductor and chip-related businesses through its “outdoor + chip” strategy.
